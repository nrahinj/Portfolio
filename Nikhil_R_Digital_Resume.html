1] ==> Integrated Planning, Structural Design, Estimation, and 3D Modeling of a Residential Building.

The project focused on the planning, drafting, and design of a G+1 residential building on a plot size of 292.78 sq.m. The work included preparation of architectural plans, structural analysis, and design of key elements such as beams, columns, slabs, and footings to ensure stability and safety. A detailed quantity take-off and cost estimation was carried out to evaluate the financial feasibility of the project. Additionally, a 3D model of the building was developed to provide a realistic visualization of the design. This project helped in gaining practical exposure to architectural planning, structural design, quantity surveying, and modern modeling tools used in civil engineering.

2] ==> Revitalization of Indrayani Riverfront : An Integrated Approach Toward Urban Development and Environmental Sustainability.

Indrayani Riverfront rejuvenation is a holistic and sustainable stepping in with the mission to improve ecological integrity, cultural heritage, and citizen participation. In bringing together of facilities such as public parks, tree planting, better ghats, pedestrian paths, and necessary services, this initiative aims to reclaim the natural character of the river while addressing the needs of inhabitants and pilgrims. Collaboration of green infrastructure, enhanced connectivity, and accessible environments generates environmental flexibility and flood mitigation potential. This redevelopment initiative looks to achieve a peaceful coexistence between urbanization and protection of the environment, making the Indrayani riverfront a lively, accessible, and spiritually enriching public space.
